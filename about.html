About Twentyone

Our story, mission, and the team behind the learning paths

Our Mission

At Twentyone, we believe that education should be structured, accessible, and relevant to real-world needs. Our mission is to provide clear, comprehensive learning paths for software developers and other professionals seeking to advance their skills in the digital economy.

We curate educational content from across the web and organize it into logical, progressive learning journeys that help you build skills methodically and effectively.

Our Story

Twentyone started in 2023 when a group of software developers, educators, and career coaches recognized a common challenge: many people struggle to navigate their learning journeys effectively, often feeling overwhelmed by the vast amount of resources available online.

We set out to create structured pathways that guide learners through complex topics step by step, ensuring they build skills in a logical sequence with clear milestones and practical projects along the way.

Twentyone is powered by a team of educators, industry professionals, and learning experience designers who are passionate about transforming professional education.
